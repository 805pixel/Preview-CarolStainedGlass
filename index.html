Our Story

Where Every
Shard Sings

Carol has spent decades mastering the ancient art of stained glass, transforming raw sheets of colored glass into luminous heirlooms that catch light in ways that make you catch your breath.

Each piece begins with a vision — yours, ours, or one inspired by the garden just outside the studio window. We craft everything by hand, from custom suncatchers to large architectural panels, garden sculptures to one-of-a-kind gifts.

This is not mass production. This is an act of devotion.
